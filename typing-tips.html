Essential Typing Tips

Home Row Position

Keep your fingers on the home row keys (ASDF for left hand, JKL; for right hand). This is your starting position for efficient typing.

Look at the Screen

Focus on the screen, not the keyboard. This helps you catch mistakes immediately and improves your typing speed.

Proper Posture

Maintain good posture: sit straight, keep your feet flat on the floor, and position your keyboard at elbow height.

Start Slow

Begin with accuracy over speed. As you become more comfortable, gradually increase your typing speed.
