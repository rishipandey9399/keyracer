Detailed Grammar Rules:

Indefinite Articles: "a" and "an"

  • Use "a" before consonant sounds: a cat, a house, a university
  • Use "an" before vowel sounds: an apple, an hour, an umbrella

Definite Article: "the"

  • Use "the" for specific items: the book on the table
  • Use "the" with superlatives: the best, the largest
