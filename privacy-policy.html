Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Key Racer! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our typing test application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using the application:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username and password.
  • Performance Data: We collect typing statistics including speed (WPM), accuracy, errors, and other metrics related to your typing performance.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the application, including test frequency, duration, and preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Track your typing progress over time
  • Provide personalized feedback to improve your typing skills
  • Improve and optimize our application
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests

4. Local Storage

Key Racer primarily uses your device's local storage (IndexedDB) to store data, including:

  • User account information
  • Typing test history and performance statistics
  • Application preferences and settings

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly choose to participate in features that require data sharing.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, you can use the account settings functionality within the application or contact us directly.

7. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can delete such information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
